What’s the Deal with Pomodoro?<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

So, what is Pomodoro? Pomodoro is a time management method develop by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s. The core idea of Pomodoro is, break your work into short, focused intervals, also known as “Pomodoros,”<\/strong> with some breaks in between.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Doing this keeps you fresh and focused for longer periods of time.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
